📥 Batch Import from Excel

Step 1
Export from OLA
Use the CSV ▾ export button on the Ledger tab. The file will have columns: Date, Debt ID, Member ID, Member Name, Amount, Balance, Note, Status.
Step 2
Upload & preview
Drop your CSV or .xlsx file here. OLA will parse each row, match Member IDs (3 digits + 1 letter, e.g. 243V), and flag duplicates automatically.
Step 3
Review & confirm
Check the preview table. Partial payments are restored from the Balance column. Click Import All to commit to the ledger.
OLA CSV Export (recommended — restores all your data)

Upload the CSV exported from OLA. All debt statuses, member names, and payment info will be restored automatically.
